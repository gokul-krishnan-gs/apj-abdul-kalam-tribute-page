Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The People's President of India

  • Born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • Key scientist in India’s missile and nuclear program.
  • Served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Authored inspiring books like Wings of Fire and Ignited Minds.
  • Known for motivating students, he said :
    “Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”
